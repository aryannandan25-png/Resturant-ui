About Khana kazana

A neighborhood restaurant celebrating bold flavors, warm hospitality, and memorable evenings.

Rooted in tradition, inspired by today.

Since 2018, Khana Kazana has offered an intimate dining room where guests can enjoy chef-driven Indian cuisine with global influences.

The menu features thoughtfully curated small plates, signature mains, and handcrafted desserts, complemented by a selection of mocktails and artisanal beverages.
